Oat Wrap


Description

This is a simple recipe I have been following since I started my weight loss journey. It is a quick and easy recipe to make your own healthy wrap that can make dieting a lot easier.

Ingredients

  • 100 gm (1 Cup) of rolled oats.
  • 200 gm of warm water
  • Half tsp of Flax Seeds
  • half tsp of Salt
  • Spices to taste

Steps

  1. Grind the oats till you reach fine-powder texture
  2. In a new bowl, add the oats and pour the water gently
  3. Whisk them together well
  4. Add the Flax Seeds and Spices (optional)
  5. After the mixture is done. Leave it to rest for 10 minutes until it thickens
  6. Heat up a non stick frying pan
  7. After you make sure that the pan is well heated, bring the heat down to medium
  8. Pour a portion of the mix into the pan and make sure it is spread evenly
  9. Wait a few minutes until the bottom of the wrap is almost done
  10. Flip the wrap and let it cook for a few more minutes.
  11. Make sure not to raise the heat, in order to avoid a burnt outside and undercooked inside
  12. Take out the wrap and put it on a kitchen towel
  13. Cover it with another towel and repeat the steps till the mix is out
